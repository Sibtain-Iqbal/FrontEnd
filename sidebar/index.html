Computer Science

Computer Science is the study of computers, programming languages, software development, networking, artificial intelligence, and data structures.

Assignments

Assignments help students to practice and apply what they learn. They improve understanding and problem-solving skills.

Quizzes

Quizzes are short tests to check your knowledge and preparation. They help in revision and learning.

Students

Students are the learners and future of technology. They participate in lectures, projects, and educational activities.
